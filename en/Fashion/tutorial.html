Guide to Using the Fashion Generation Feature

The Fashion Generation feature in AiCasso lets you dress someone in a specific outfit, such as a shirt, dress, or pants. Simply describe what you want, and watch AiCasso apply the clothing item to the person in the image.

Optional Parameters

If you want to add your own style or make sure the result is just right, you can tweak these settings:

  • Negative Prompt: If there's something you don’t want in the image, just mention it here. Leave it blank if you’re cool with whatever AiCasso comes up with.
  • Cloth Type: Tell AiCasso whether you’re focusing on the upper body, lower body, or dresses. If you're not sure, it defaults to upper body.
  • Number of Denoising Steps: This decides how smooth and polished the image looks. You can choose between 21, 31, or 41 steps, with 21 being the default. More steps usually mean a more refined look.
  • Guidance Scale: This helps the image stick closely to your description. You can set it from 1 to 20, with 7 being the default. Higher numbers mean AiCasso will follow your prompt more closely.

Remember, these settings are optional! AiCasso is pretty smart and can figure out what you mean even if you don't get every detail perfect. Just have fun and let your creativity flow!
