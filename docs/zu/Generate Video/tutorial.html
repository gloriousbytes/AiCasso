Izinketho Ezengeziwe

Ufuna ukuhlela ividiyo yakho? Nansi eminye imingcele ongayidlala ngayo:

  • Inani Lezithombe: Khetha ukuthi zingaki izithombe (izithombe ezihlukile) ividiyo yakho izoba nazo. Ungaya ku-25 izithombe, kanti u-16 ungumjikelezo ojwayelekile.
  • Inani Lezinyathelo Zokunciphisa Umsindo: Lokhu kulawula ukuthi ividiyo yakho ibukeka kanjani. Izinyathelo eziningi zisho ama-vidiyo ahlanzekile. Ungasetha kuze kube yizinyathelo eziyi-50, kanti u-20 ungumjikelezo ojwayelekile.
  • Isikali Sokuhola: Lokhu kusiza ividiyo ukuba ihlale ikhuluma kahle nencazelo yakho. Njengoba inani liphakeme, kunjalo futhi ilandela isikhumbuzo sakho. Umkhawulo ungama-0 kuya ku-8, kanti u-8 ungumjikelezo ojwayelekile.
  • Ukweqa I-Clip: Phonsa ezinye izingqimba ukuze uthole isitayela sevidiyo esihlukile. Ngokuvamile, akukho zingqimba ezishiywe, kodwa ungashiya kuze kube yizimbili.
  • Ububanzi Nobude Bokwandisa: Ungenza ividiyo yakho ibe nkulu ngokulungisa isixazululo. Umjikelezo ojwayelekile ungama-1024x1024 pixels, kodwa ungakwenza lokhu ukuze kuhlangabezane nezidingo zakho.
  • Ubukhulu Bokwandisa: Lokhu kushintsha ukuthi ividiyo yakho ilungiswe kangakanani uma ikhuliswa. Umjikelezo ojwayelekile ungama-1, kodwa ungakwenza kube nokuhluka okuningi.
  • Isikali Sokuhola Sokwandisa: Lokhu kuhola inqubo yokwandisa, enomkhawulo ovela ku-0 kuya ku-8, futhi umjikelezo ojwayelekile ungama-8.
  • Izinyathelo Zokuhlola Ukukhulisa: Inani lezinyathelo ezithathwayo ukuze kukhuliswe ividiyo. Izinyathelo eziningi zenza ukukhulisa kube lula, kanti umkhawulo ungama-50 no-20 ungumjikelezo ojwayelekile.
  • Ukuthola Okuthuthukisiwe: Ufuna ividiyo ethuthukisiwe? Vula lokhu. Kuvalwe ngokujwayelekile.
  • Isikhumbuzo Sokuthola Okuthuthukisiwe: Sebenzisa lokhu ukuze udale ividiyo efanayo njalo ngokuqhubekayo. Kukhethwa futhi kuvalwe ngokujwayelekile.
  • Izithombe Ngomzuzu (FPS): Setha ijubane levidiyo yakho. Umjikelezo ojwayelekile ungama-16 FPS, kodwa ungakwenza kube phezulu ku-16.
  • Imodeli Yevidiyo: Khetha isitayela sevidiyo yakho. Izinketho zifaka:
    • Ejwayelekile: Imodeli yevidiyo ejwayelekile.
    • I-Cartoon: Isitayela sevidiyo esifana ne-cartoon.
    • Yemvelo: Isitayela sevidiyo esibukeka sengathi semvelo.
    • I-Animated 3D: Isitayela sevidiyo esihlanganisa i-3D.

Khumbula, lezi zinketho zikhethwa. Isici Sokwakha Ividiyo sisebenza kahle ngokuchaza okulula, ngakho uzizwe ukhululekile ukuzama futhi ubone lokho ongakwenza!
