Izinketho Ezengeziwe

Ufuna ukulungisa ividiyo yakho? Nansi eminye imingcele ongayidlala ngayo:

  • Inani Lezithombe: Khetha ukuthi zingaki izithombe (izithombe ezihlukile) ividiyo yakho izoba nazo. Ungaya ku-25 izithombe, kanti u-16 ungowokungena.
  • Inani Lezinyathelo Zokunciphisa: Lokhu kulawula ukuthi ividiyo yakho ibukeka kanjani. Izinyathelo eziningi zisho ama-vidiyo ahlanzekile. Ungasetha kuze kube yizinyathelo eziyi-50, kanti u-20 ungowokungena.
  • Isikali Sokuhola: Lokhu kusiza ividiyo ukuthi ihlale ibambelela encazelweni yakho. Njengoba inani liphakeme, kakhulukazi ilandela ukukhuthaza kwakho. Ibanga lingu-0 kuya ku-8, kanti u-8 ungowokungena.
  • Ukweqa Iziqeshana: Ukweqa ezinye izingqimba ukuze uthole isitayela sevidiyo esihlukile. Ngokujwayelekile, akukho zingqimba ezikweqwayo, kodwa ungakweqa kuze kube yizimbili.
  • Ukukhulisa Ububanzi Nobude: Ungenza ividiyo yakho ibe nkulu ngokulungisa isixazululo. Okungokujwayelekile ngu-1024x1024 pixels, kodwa ungakwenza lokhu ukuze kuhambisane nezidingo zakho.
  • Ukuqinisa Ukukhulisa: Lokhu kushintsha ukuthi ividiyo yakho ilungiswe kangakanani uma ikhuliswe. Okungokujwayelekile ngu-1, kodwa ungakwenza ukuze ubone umehluko omningi.
  • Isikali Sokuhola Sokukhulisa: Lokhu kuhola inqubo yokukhulisa, ibanga lingu-0 kuya ku-8, futhi okungokujwayelekile ngu-8.
  • Izinyathelo Zokuhlola Ukukhulisa: Inani lezinyathelo ezithathwayo ukuze kukhuliswe ividiyo. Izinyathelo eziningi zenza ukukhulisa kube smoother, kanti inani eliphezulu ngu-50 kanti okungokujwayelekile ngu-20.
  • Ukuthola Okuthuthukisiwe: Ufuna ividiyo ethuthukisiwe? Vula lokhu. Kuvalwe ngokujwayelekile.
  • Imbewu Yokuthola Okuthuthukisiwe: Sebenzisa lokhu ukuze udale ividiyo efanayo njalo ngenkathi uthola okuthuthukisiwe. Kukhethwa futhi kuvalwe ngokujwayelekile.
  • Izithombe Ngomzuzu (FPS): Setha ispeeds yevidiyo yakho. Okungokujwayelekile ngu-16 FPS, kodwa ungakwenza kube phezulu ku-16.
  • Imodeli Yevidiyo: Khetha isitayela sevidiyo yakho. Izinketho zifaka:
    • Okungokujwayelekile: Imodeli yevidiyo ejwayelekile.
    • I-Cartoon: Isitayela sevidiyo esifana ne-cartoon.
    • Imvelo: Isitayela sevidiyo esibukeka sengokoqobo, semvelo.
    • 3D Ehlakaniphile: Isitayela sevidiyo esihlanganisa i-3D.

Khumbula, lezi zinketho zikhethwa. Isici Sokwakha Ividiyo sisebenza kahle ngokuchaza okulula, ngakho uzizwe ukhululekile ukujola futhi ubone lokho ongakwenza!
