Izilungiselelo Ezikhethwayo

Uma ufuna ukuba nemininingwane ethile kuvidiyo yakho, nansi eminye imingcele ongayilungisa:

  • Imiyalo Engalungile: Chaza lokho *ongafuni* ukukubona kuvidiyo. Uma kushiywe ingenalutho, i-AiCasso izogxila kuphela kumiyalo emihle.
  • Inani LamaFrames: Khetha ukuthi mangaki amaframes (izithombe ezihlukene) ividiyo yakho izoba nazo. Ungakhetha kuze kube ngu-25 amaframes, kanti u-16 ungumkhawulo ojwayelekile.
  • Izinyathelo Zokuhlanza: Lokhu kulawula ukuthi ividiyo yakho ibukeka kanjani. Izinyathelo eziningi zisho ividiyo ehlanzekile. Ungasetha kuze kube ngu-50 izinyathelo, kanti u-20 ungumkhawulo ojwayelekile.
  • Isikali Sokuhola: Lokhu kusiza ividiyo ukuthi ihlale ibambelela encazelweni yakho. Umkhawulo usuka ku-1.0 kuya ku-8.0, futhi ungakwazi ukulungisa amanani okuqala nokugcina ukuze uqondise ukuthi ividiyo ithuthuka kanjani.
  • Ukunyakaza: Lungisa ukuthi kukhona kangakanani ukuhamba kuvidiyo yakho. Inani eliphezulu lisho ukuhamba okuningi. Umkhawulo usuka ku-0 kuya ku-127, kanti u-60 ungumkhawulo ojwayelekile.
  • Ubukhulu Bokukh noise: Engeza umsindo kuvidiyo yakho ukuze ube nokubukeka okungaphezulu. Umkhawulo usuka ku-0 kuya ku-1, kanti u-0 ungumkhawulo ojwayelekile.
  • AmaFrames Ngomzuzu (FPS): Setha ukuthi ividiyo yakho idlalwe ngokushesha kangakanani. Umkhawulo ojwayelekile ngu-16 FPS, kodwa ungaya ku-16 ukuze udlale ngokushesha.
  • Indlela Yokuhlanganisa: Khetha ukuthi amaframes ahlanganiswa kanjani. Umkhawulo ojwayelekile ngu-"lerp" (ukuhlanganiswa okujwayelekile), kodwa ungasebenzisa futhi "slerp" (ukuhlanganiswa kwe-spherical linear) ukuze uthole imiphumela ehlukene.
  • Ukushiya I-Clip: Shiya ezinye izingqimba ukuze uthole isitayela sevidiyo esihlukile. Ungashiya kuze kube yizingqimba ezi-2, noma uyishiye ingenalutho ukuze ube nesimo esijwayelekile.
