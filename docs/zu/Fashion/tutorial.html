Umhlahlandlela Wokusebenzisa Isici Sokugqoka

Isici sokugqoka ku-AiCasso sikuvumela ukuthi ugqoke umuntu ngempahla ethile, njengeshati, ijezi, noma amabhulukwe. Chaza kahle lokho okufunayo, bese ubuka i-AiCasso ifaka le mpahla kumuntu osembonweni.

Izinketho Ezingeziwe

Uma ufuna ukwengeza isitayela sakho noma uqinisekise ukuthi umphumela uhlelekile, ungashintsha lezi zinketho:

  • Isikhumbuzo Esibi: Uma kukhona okuthile ongafuni ukuba kusithombeni, vele ukhulume lapha. Shiya ingenalutho uma ukhululekile nganoma yini i-AiCasso eyakha.
  • Uhlobo Lwephethini: Tshela i-AiCasso ukuthi ugxile emzimbeni phezulu, phansi, noma ijazi. Uma ungazi, izoba phezulu ngokuzenzakalelayo.
  • Inani Lezinyathelo Zokunciphisa Umsindo: Lokhu kunquma ukuthi isithombe sibukeka kanjani sithambile futhi sikhanyisiwe. Ungakhetha phakathi kwezinyathelo eziyi-21, eziyi-31, noma eziyi-41, lapho eziyi-21 ziba yizinga elizenzakalelayo. Izinyathelo eziningi ngokuvamile zisho ukubukeka okucacile.
  • Isikali Sokuhola: Lokhu kusiza isithombe sibe seduze kakhulu nencazelo yakho. Ungasetha kusuka ku-1 kuya ku-20, lapho u-7 engumkhawulo. Izinombolo eziphezulu zisho ukuthi i-AiCasso izolandela isikhumbuzo sakho ngokucophelela.

Khumbula, lezi zinketho ziyizinketho ezikhethwayo! I-AiCasso ikhululekile futhi ingakwazi ukuqonda lokho okukhuluma ngakho noma ungatholi yonke imininingwane kahle. Jabulela futhi uvumele ubuciko bakho buhamba!
