Umhlahlandlela Wokusebenzisa Isici Sokugqoka

Isici sokugqoka ku-AiCasso sikuvumela ukuthi ugqoke umuntu ngempahla ethile, njengehembe, ibhulukwe, noma amabhulukwe. Chaza kahle lokho okufunayo, bese ubuka i-AiCasso ifaka le mpahla kumuntu osembonweni.

Izinketho Ezingeziwe

Uma ufuna ukwengeza isitayela sakho noma uqinisekise ukuthi umphumela ulungile, ungashintsha lezi zinketho:

  • Isikhumbuzo Esibi: Uma kukhona okuthile ongafuni ukuba kusithombeni, vele ukhulume lapha. Shiya ingenalutho uma uhamba kahle nalokho okukhona ku-AiCasso.
  • Uhlobo Lwekhothi: Tshela i-AiCasso ukuthi ugxile emzimbeni phezulu, phansi, noma emibhalweni. Uma ungazi, izoba phezulu njengokujwayelekile.
  • Inani Lezinyathelo Zokunciphisa Umsindo: Lokhu kukhetha ukuthi isithombe sibukeka kanjani sithambile futhi sikhanyisiwe. Ungakhetha phakathi kwezinyathelo eziyi-21, 31, noma 41, lapho eziyi-21 ziba yizijwayelekile. Izinyathelo eziningi ngokuvamile zisho ukubukeka okucacile.
  • Isikali Sokuhola: Lokhu kusiza isithombe sibe seduze kakhulu nencazelo yakho. Ungasetha kusuka ku-1 kuya ku-20, lapho u-7 engumjwayelo. Izinombolo eziphezulu zisho ukuthi i-AiCasso izolandela isikhumbuzo sakho ngokucophelela.

Khumbula, lezi zinketho ziyizinketho! I-AiCasso iyahlakanipha kakhulu futhi ingathola lokho okukhuluma nakuba ungatholi yonke imininingwane kahle. Jabulela futhi uvumele ubuciko bakho buhamba!
