Amamojula Okukhethwa

Uma ufuna ukwengeza ubuntu obuningi noma ukulawula ezithombeni zakho ezakhiwe, nansi eminye imiklamo ongayiguqula:

  • Umusho Ophiwe: Lapha ungatshela i-AiCasso ukuthi yini ongafuni ukuyibona esithombeni. Uma uyishiya ingenalutho, i-AiCasso izogxila kumusho omuhle kuphela.
  • Inani Lezinyathelo Zokuhlanza: Lokhu kulawula ukuthi isithombe sibukeka kanjani sithambile futhi sihle. Ungakhetha phakathi kwezinyathelo eziyi-21, eziyi-31, noma eziyi-41, kanti eziyi-41 ziyi-default. Izinyathelo eziningi zisho isithombe esihlanzekile, esihlotshisiwe.
  • Isikali Sokuhola: Lokhu kusiza isithombe sibe ngokoqobo kulokho okuchazayo. Isikali sishintsha kusuka ku-1 kuya ku-20, kanti i-10 iyinani elijwayelekile. Izinombolo eziphezulu zisho ukuthi i-AiCasso izogcina ibhandele umusho wakho.
  • S Scale: Lokhu kuhlobene nesakhiwo sobuso. Kukhona okuthile kwezobuchwepheshe, kodwa ngokuyinhloko kulungisa isisindo sokwakheka kobuso. I-default ingu-1.0, futhi ungaya noma kuphi kusuka ku-0.0 kuya ku-2.0.
  • Isitayela: Ufuna ukuhlinzeka ngesithombe sakho ngesimo esithile? Ungakhetha isitayela esifana ne-comic, chibi, 3D, anime, noma esibonakalayo. Esibonakalayo iyinani elijwayelekile, kodwa uzizwe ukhululekile ukuba nekhono!

Futhi khumbula, i-AiCasso iyaxolela. Awudingi ukuba ube nguchwepheshe kwezokubhala noma uthole yonke imininingwane iphelele. Chaza kuphela lokho okufunayo ngamagama akho, futhi i-AiCasso izobhekana nezinye izinto. Jabulela ukudala!
