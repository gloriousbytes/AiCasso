Izinketho Ezingeziwe

Uma ufuna ukwengeza okuthile kokuphila noma ukulawula ezithombeni zakho ezikhiqizwayo, nansi eminye imikhawulo ongayishintsha:

  • Ukuphakamisa Okubi: Lapha ungatshela i-AiCasso ukuthi yini ongafuni ukuyibona esithombeni. Uma uyishiya ingenalutho, i-AiCasso izogxila kuphela kokuphakamisa okuhle.
  • Inani Lezinyathelo Zokunciphisa Umsindo: Lokhu kulawula ukuthi isithombe sibukeka kanjani sithambile futhi sithuthukile. Ungakhetha phakathi kwezinyathelo ezingu-21, 31, noma 41, lapho 41 kuyinto ejwayelekile. Izinyathelo eziningi zisho isithombe esihlanzekile, esihlotshisiwe.
  • Isikali Sokuhola: Lokhu kusiza isithombe sibe seqinile kulokho okuchazayo. Isikali sikhula ukusuka ku-1 kuya ku-20, lapho u-10 kuyinto ejwayelekile. Izibalo eziphezulu zisho ukuthi i-AiCasso izogcina ibhandele kakhulu kokuphakamisa kwakho.
  • S Scale: Lokhu kuhlobene nesakhiwo sobuso. Kukhona okuthile kwezobuchwepheshe, kodwa ngokuyinhloko kulungisa isisindo sokwakheka kobuso. I-default ingu-1.0, futhi ungaya noma kuphi ukusuka ku-0.0 kuya ku-2.0.
  • Isitayela: Ufuna ukuhlinzeka ngesithombe sakho ngesimo esithile? Ungakhetha isitayela esifana ne-comic, chibi, 3D, anime, noma esibonakalayo. Esibonakalayo kuyinto ejwayelekile, kodwa uzizwe ukhululekile ukuba nekhono!

Futhi khumbula, i-AiCasso iyaxolela. Awudingi ukuba ngumlingani wezokubhala noma uthole yonke imininingwane iphelele. Chaza kuphela lokho okufunayo ngamagama akho, futhi i-AiCasso izobhekana nezinye izinto. Jabulela ukudala!
