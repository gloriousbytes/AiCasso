Umhlahlandlela Wokusebenzisa Isici Sokwandisa

Isici sokwandisa ku-AiCasso sikhuluma ngokwenza izithombe zakho zibe ngcono. Uveza nje ukuthi ufuna kanjani ukuthuthukisa isithombe sakho, futhi i-AiCasso izobhekana nezinye izinto. Kungakhathaliseki ukuthi uthuthukisa imininingwane noma unikeza isithombe sonke ukwanda, kulula kakhulu ukusisebenzisa.

Izinketho Ezingeziwe

Uma ufuna ukwengeza ukulawula okwengeziwe ngendlela isithombe sakho sithuthukiswa ngayo, nansi eminye imisebenzi ongayisebenzisa:

  • Isikali: Lokhu kuwukuthi ungafuna ukwandisa isithombe sakho kangakanani. Ungakhetha isikali esivela ku-1 kuya ku-4, lapho u-4 engumkhiqizo ojwayelekile. Ngamanye amazwi, izinombolo ezinkulu zisho isithombe esikhulu.
  • Imodeli Yokwandisa: Lokhu kufana nokukhetha ifilitha yesithombe sakho, kodwa ikhuluma kakhulu. Nansi eminye imisebenzi:
    • Ukukhuphula Imininingwane: Okungcono kakhulu uma ufuna ukugxila eminininingwaneni emincane.
    • Ultra Sharp: Yenza isithombe sibe sobala futhi sibe nekhwalithi ephezulu.
    • Anime Enhance: Kulungile ezithombeni ezinomoya we-anime, zenze zibe zicace futhi zikhangayo.
    • Double Detail: Ikhuphula imininingwane ngaphandle kokweqa umkhawulo.
    • General Boost: Inketho enhle yokujwayelekile yezithombe eziningi.
  • Face Enhance: Uma isithombe sakho sinefayela, le nketho ingasiza ukwenza kube ngcono. Vula uma ufuna i-AiCasso igxile ekuthuthukiseni izici zobuso.
