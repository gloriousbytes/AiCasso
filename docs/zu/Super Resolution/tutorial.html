Umhlahlandlela Wokusebenzisa Isici Sokwandisa

Isici sokwandisa ku-AiCasso sikhuluma ngokuqinisekisa ukuthi izithombe zakho zibonakala zinhle kakhulu. Uveza nje ukuthi ufuna kanjani ukuthuthukisa isithombe sakho, futhi i-AiCasso izobheka konke okunye. Kungakhathaliseki ukuthi uthuthukisa imininingwane noma unikeza isithombe sonke ukukhuphuka, kulula kakhulu ukusisebenzisa.

Izinketho Ezingeziwe

Uma ufuna ukwengeza ukulawula okwengeziwe ngendlela isithombe sakho sithuthukiswa ngayo, nansi eminye imisebenzi ongayisebenzisa:

  • Isikali: Lokhu kuwukuthi ungafuna kangakanani ukwandisa isithombe sakho. Ungakhetha isikali esivela ku-1 kuya ku-4, lapho u-4 engumsebenzi ojwayelekile. Ngamafuphi, izinombolo ezinkulu zisho isithombe esikhulu.
  • Imodeli Yokwandisa: Lokhu kufana nokukhetha ifilitha yesithombe sakho, kodwa ikakhulukazi iyahlakanipha. Nansi eminye imisebenzi:
    • Ukukhuphula Imininingwane Enkulu: Kuhle kakhulu uma uhamba eduze kwemisebenzi emincane.
    • Ultra Sharp: Kwenzela isithombe sibe sobala futhi sibe nekhwalithi ephezulu.
    • Anime Enhance: Kuhle kakhulu ezithombeni ze-anime, zenze zibe zicace futhi zibe nezinhlobonhlobo.
    • Double Detail: Ikhuphula imininingwane kabili ngaphandle kokweqa.
    • General Boost: Inketho enhle yokujwayelekile yezithombe eziningi.
  • Face Enhance: Uma isithombe sakho sinezindawo zobuso, le nketho ingasiza ukwenza kube ngcono. Vula uma ufuna i-AiCasso igxile ekuthuthukiseni izici zobuso.
