  • Digital Art: Gxila kumsebenzi wobuciko obuhlanzekile, obupholile.
  • Fantasy Art: Gcizelela izithombe ezinemininingwane, ezikhuthazwa ubuciko be-fantasy.
  • Isometric: Sebenzisa umbono we-isometric ukuze udale ukubukeka kwemodeli ye-3D.
  • Line Art: Gxila kumigqa ehlanzekile, ehlanzekile ukuze udale imidwebo enemininingwane.
  • Low Poly: Sebenzisa imodeli ye-3D ye-low-polygon ukuze udale ukubukeka okuncane, okukhanyayo.
  • Neonpunk: Hlanganisa ukukhanya kwe-neon nezithombe eziphindaphindayo, ezithokozisayo ze-cyberpunk.
  • Origami: Gxila kumklamo onemininingwane, ophindaphindayo ojwayelekile we-origami.
  • Photographic: Uhlosile ukufinyelela izithombe eziphilayo, eziphilayo.
  • Pixel Art: Sebenzisa izithombe eziphindaphindayo ukuze udale ukubukeka okukhumbulekayo, okukhumbulekayo.
  • Texture: Gxila ezithombeni ezinemininingwane, eziphilayo ukuze uthuthukise ukujula kwezithombe.
