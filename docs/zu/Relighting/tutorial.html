Izinguquko Ezikhethwayo

Uma uzizwa ungena emhlabeni omusha futhi ufuna ukwengeza okuthile okujabulisayo ekukhanyiseni kwakho, nansi eminye imicabango ongayiguqula:

  • Imiyalo Engalungile: Ufuna ukugwema okuthile? Ungatshela i-AiCasso ukuthi ungafaki ini. Kodwa ungakhathazeki, uma uyishiya ingenalutho, i-AiCasso izogxila kulokho okufunayo.
  • Umgomo Lokukhanya: Ungachaza ukuthi ukukhanya kufanele kuqhamuke kuphi—kwesokunxele, kwesokudla, phezulu, phansi, noma ngisho nakuwo wonke. Ngokujwayelekile, ukukhanya kuvela kwesokunxele.
  • Isikali Sokuhola: Lokhu kusiza i-AiCasso ukuthi igcine incazelo yakho. Isikali sihamba ukusuka ku-1 kuya ku-20, lapho u-7 engowejwayelekile. Njengoba inombolo iphakama, i-AiCasso igcina kakhulu kumyalo wakho.
  • Isikali Esiphezulu Sokuxilonga: Ufuna isithombe sakho sikhanye? Lokhu kulawula ukujula. Okujwayelekile ngu-1.5, kodwa ungakwenza kube phakathi kuka-1.0 no-2.0 ukuze uthole isithombe esicacile.
  • Izinga Lokunciphisa Umsindo: Kukhona amazinga amabili—okuphansi nokuphakeme. Lokhu kulawula ukuthi isithombe sokugcina sibukeka kanjani. Ungasetha izinga lokunciphisa umsindo ophansi phakathi kuka-0.0 no-1.0 (okujwayelekile ngu-0.9) kanye nezinga eliphezulu phakathi kuka-0.0 no-1.0 (okujwayelekile ngu-0.5).
  • Inani Lezinyathelo: Lokhu kuwukuthi zingaki izinyathelo i-AiCasso ezithatha ukuze ithuthukise isithombe sakho. Okujwayelekile ngu-50, kodwa ungahamba ukusuka ku-20 kuya ku-50 izinyathelo.

Khumbula, lezi zinguquko zikhululekile! I-AiCasso ikhululekile ekutholeni ukuthi ufisa ini, ngakho chaza umbono wakho ngezilimi zakho, futhi izokwenza um magic. Akudingeki ukuba ube negrama noma imininingwane ephelele—jabulani futhi ubone ukuthi ungakha ini!
