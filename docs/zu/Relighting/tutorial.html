Izinguquko Ezikhethwayo

Uma uzizwa ungena emhlabeni futhi ufuna ukwengeza okuthile okukhanyayo ekukhanyiseni kwakho, nansi eminye imicabango ongayiguqula:

  • Imiyalo Engalungile: Ufuna ukuvikela okuthile? Ungatshela i-AiCasso ukuthi ungafaki ini. Kodwa ungakhathazeki, uma uyishiya ingenalutho, i-AiCasso izogxila kulokho okufunayo.
  • Umgomo Lokukhanya: Ungachaza ukuthi ukukhanya kufanele kuqhamuke kuphi—kwesobunxele, kwesokudla, phezulu, phansi, noma ngisho kungabi khona. Ngokuvamile, ukukhanya kuvela kwesobunxele.
  • Isikali Sokuhola: Lokhu kusiza i-AiCasso ukuthi igcine incazelo yakho. Isikali sihamba kusuka ku-1 kuya ku-20, lapho u-7 engumkhawulo. Njengoba inombolo iphakama, i-AiCasso igcina incazelo yakho kakhulu.
  • Isikali Esiphezulu Sokuxilonga: Ufuna isithombe sakho sikhanye? Lokhu kulawula ukujula. Umkhawulo ungama-1.5, kodwa ungawusetha kusuka ku-1.0 kuya ku-2.0 ukuze uthole isithombe esicacile.
  • Izinga Lokunciphisa Umsindo: Kukhona amazinga amabili—okuphansi nokuphakeme. Lokhu kulawula ukuthi isithombe sokugcina sibe bushelelezi noma sibe nemininingwane. Ungasetha izinga lokunciphisa umsindo ophansi phakathi kuka-0.0 no-1.0 (umkhawulo ungama-0.9) kanye nezinga eliphezulu phakathi kuka-0.0 no-1.0 (umkhawulo ungama-0.5).
  • Inani Lezinyathelo: Lokhu kuwukuthi zingaki izinyathelo i-AiCasso ezithatha ukuze ithuthukise isithombe sakho. Umkhawulo ungama-50, kodwa ungahamba kusuka ku-20 kuya ku-50 izinyathelo.
