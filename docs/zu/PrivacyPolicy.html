Umthetho Wobumfihlo we-AiCasso

Usuku Lokusebenza: Septhemba 13, 2024

Kwa-AiCasso, ubumfihlo bakho buwumgomo wethu. Lo Mthetho Wobumfihlo uchaza ukuthi silawula kanjani ulwazi oluhlinzekwa uma usebenzisa uhlelo lwethu lokusebenza.

  1. Ulwazi Esiluhlanganisayo
    • Izithombe Ezifakiwe: Siqoqe izithombe ozifakayo ku-AiCasso kuphela ukuze sinikeze izinsizakalo zethu.
    • Idatha Yobuso: Uma ufaka isithombe esinezinhlamvu zobuso, sihlela izici zobuso ukuze sithuthukise izithombe ezenziwe ngokwezifiso ezisekelwe ezicini ezikhethiwe ngumsebenzisi. Le datha yobuso ikhishwa ezithombeni zakho ezifakiwe futhi isetshenziselwa kuphela inhloso yokwakha izithombe.
  2. Indlela Esisebenzisa Ngayo Ulwazi Lwakho
    • Ukuhlinzeka Ngensizakalo: Izithombe ezifakiwe, kuhlanganise nanoma iyiphi idatha yobuso, zisetshenziswa kuphela ukwenza imisebenzi enikezwa yi-AiCasso. Lokhu kuhlanganisa ukukhiqiza izithombe ezintsha ezisekelwe ezicini ozikhethayo.
  3. Ukugcina Idatha
    • Ukususa Ngokushesha Izithombe Ezifakiwe: Zonke izithombe ezifakiwe ziyasuswa ngokushesha ngemva kokuthi zisebenze futhi inhloso yazo ifezwe.
    • Ukugcina Kwesikhashana Izithombe Ezikhiqiziwe: Izithombe ezikhiqiziwe zigcinwa kusevisi yethu ye-cloud futhi zikhona kumsebenzisi isikhathi esingama-24 amahora. Lezi zithombe ziyasuswa ngokuzenzakalelayo ngemuva kwalesi sikhathi.
    • Ukuphathwa Kwe-Cloud Storage: Ukugcina kwethu kwe-cloud kuhlanzwa njalo njalo emavikini angama-24 ukuze kuqinisekiswe ukuthi akukho datha esele.
  4. Ukuxhumana Ngemininingwane
    • Akukho Ukuxhumana Nezithathu: Asabelani, asithengisi, noma asivezi izithombe zakho, idatha yobuso, noma yimuphi ulwazi lomuntu siqu kwabanye. Wonke umsebenzi wedatha uphathwa ngaphakathi kwe-AiCasso.
  5. Izinyathelo Zokuphepha
    • Ukuvikelwa Kwedatha: Sisebenzisa izinyathelo zokuphepha ezijwayelekile embonini ukuvikela izithombe zakho nedatha yobuso ngesikhathi sokucubungula nasekugcinweni kusevisi yethu ye-cloud.
  6. Izinguquko Kulo Mthetho Wobumfihlo
    • Izibuyekezo Zomthetho: Singase sithuthukise lo Mthetho Wobumfihlo ngezikhathi ezithile. Noma yiziphi izinguquko zizoboniswa ngedethi yokusebenza evuselelwe.
