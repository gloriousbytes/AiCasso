Amathiphu Wokuhlela Ngemifanekiso

Isici se-Image Guided Edit ku-AiCasso sikuvumela ukuthi wenze izinguquko emfanekisweni ngokuchaza lokho okufunayo. Noma ngabe ufuna ukushintsha isitayela, ulungise umoya, noma uthuthukise izingxenye ezithile, i-AiCasso izokwenza lokho kuwe ngaphandle kokuthi udinge ukukhetha noma ukucaba noma yini ngesandla.

Ukusebenza Kwayo:

Isithombe Sokuqala:
Chaza Ushintsho Lwakho:

Faka nje lokho okufunayo ukushintsha, futhi i-AiCasso izofaka lezo zinguquko emfanekisweni.

Izibonelo Zokuhlela:

Isibonelo 1: "Yenza abe nezinwele ezimhlophe."

Umphumela: I-AiCasso izoshintsha umbala wezinwele zomuntu ube mhlophe.

Isibonelo 2: "Yenza kube nobusuku, sebenzisa izinyathelo eziyi-50, isikali sokuhola esingu-1, kanye nesikali sokuhola kwemifanekiso esingu-1."
Isithombe Sokufaka:

Umphumela: Isithombe sizoguqulwa sibe yindawo yobusuku enezinguquko ezithile ezisekelwe emininingwaneni oyinikezile.

Izinketho Ezengeziwe

Ungathuthukisa ukuhlela kwakho usebenzisa lezi zinketho ezikhethwayo:

  • Isikali Sokuhola Imifanekiso: Lokhu kulawula ukuthi isithombe esisha sithanda kangakanani isithombe sokuqala. Inani eliphansi lisho ukuthi izinguquko zinciphile, kanti inani eliphezulu lisho ukuthi zivele kakhulu. Ungakhetha phakathi kuka-1 no-8.
  • Izinyathelo: Lokhu kulawula ukuthi zingaki izilungiselelo ithuluzi lizokwenza. Izinyathelo eziningi zisho isithombe esihambisanayo nesichazayo. Ungakhetha phakathi kuka-1 no-50, kanti u-20 ungumkhawulo ojwayelekile.
  • Isikali Sokuhola: Lokhu kuthinta ukuthi i-AI ilandela kanjani incazelo yakho. Isikali esiphansi sinika i-AI inkululeko yokudala, kanti isikali esiphezulu sithinta kakhulu lokho okukhuluma. Ungakwenza lokhu phakathi kuka-1 no-20.
