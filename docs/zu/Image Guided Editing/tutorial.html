Amathiphu Wokuhlela Ngemifanekiso

Isici sokuhlela ngemifanekiso ku-AiCasso sikuvumela ukuthi wenze izinguquko emfanekisweni ngokuchaza kuphela lokho okufunayo. Noma ngabe ufuna ukushintsha isitayela, ulungise umoya, noma ushintshe izingxenye ezithile, i-AiCasso izokwenza lokho kuwe ngaphandle kokuthi udinge ukukhetha noma uphinde uphinde noma yini.

Ukusebenza Kwayo:

Isithombe Sokuqala:
Chaza Ushintsho Lwakho:

Bhala nje lokho okufunayo ukushintsha, futhi i-AiCasso izosebenzisa lezi zinguquko emfanekisweni.

Izibonelo Zokuhlela:

Isibonelo 1: "Yenza abe nezinwele ezimhlophe."

Umphumela: I-AiCasso izoshintsha umbala wezinwele zomuntu ube mhlophe.

Isibonelo 2: "Yenza kube busuku, sebenzisa izinyathelo eziyi-50, isikali sokuhola esingu-1, kanye nesikali sokuhola kwemifanekiso esingu-1."
Isithombe Sokufaka:

Umphumela: Isithombe sizoguqulwa sibe yisimo sobusuku ngezinguquko ezithile ezisekelwe emikhiqizweni oyinikezile.

Izinketho Ezengeziwe

Ungakwazi ukuhlela kahle izinguquko zakho usebenzisa lezi zinketho ezikhethwayo:

  • Isikali Sokuhola Kwenkanyezi: Lokhu kulawula ukuthi isithombe esisha sithanda kangakanani isithombe sokuqala. Inani eliphansi lisho ukuthi izinguquko zinciphile, kanti inani eliphezulu lisho ukuthi zivelele kakhulu. Ungakhetha phakathi kuka-1 no-8.
  • Izinyathelo: Lokhu kulawula ukuthi zingaki izinguquko ezizokwenziwa ithuluzi. Izinyathelo eziningi zisho isithombe esihamba kahle futhi esinemininingwane. Ungakhetha phakathi kuka-1 no-50, kanti u-20 uyisethaphu esijwayelekile.
  • Isikali Sokuhola: Lokhu kuthinta ukuthi i-AI ilandela kanjani incazelo yakho. Isikali esiphansi sinika i-AI inkululeko yokudala, kanti isikali esiphezulu sithinta kakhulu lokho okukhuluma. Ungakwenza lokhu phakathi kuka-1 no-20.
