Imigomo Yokusebenzisa i-AiCasso

Usuku Lokusebenza: Septhemba 13, 2024

Wamukelekile ku-AiCasso, umakhi wezithombe ophakanyiswe yi-AI. Ngokufinyelela noma ukusebenzisa uhlelo lwethu, uyavuma ukuhambisana nokubophela kulezi Migomo Yokusebenzisa.

  1. Ukwamukela Imigomo
    • Ngokusebenzisa i-AiCasso, uyavuma le Migomo Yokusebenzisa kanye neNqubomgomo Yokuvikela. Uma ungavumi, sicela ungasebenzisi izinsizakalo zethu.
  2. Ukusetshenziswa Kwehlelo
    • Ungasebenzisa i-AiCasso ngezinhloso ezisemthethweni futhi ngokuhambisana nale Migomo.
    • Uyavuma ukuthi ungasebenzisi uhlelo ngendlela engase ibangele ukulimala noma ukuphazamiseka kohlelo noma ukuphazamiseka kokusetshenziswa komunye umuntu.
  3. Okuqukethwe Okwenziwe Ngabasebenzisi
    • Uphendula kuphela ngezithombe ozikhiqizayo kanye nemiphumela yokwabelana ngazo.
    • Uveza futhi uqinisekisa ukuthi unamalungelo noma unamalungelo adingekayo ukuze usebenzise futhi wabelane ngokuqukethwe okukhishwe.
    • Ugcina wonke amalungelo okuba neziqu zezithombe ozikhiqizayo usebenzisa i-AiCasso.
  4. Imihlahlandlela Yokuhlonza Okuqukethwe
    • Uyavuma ukuthi ungakhiqizi noma ungabelani ngokuqukethwe okuthi:
      • Kungemthetho, kuyingozi, kuyasongela, kuyabhalwa, kuyahlasela, kuyadumaza, kuyahlambalaza, noma kuphula ubumfihlo bomunye.
      • Kuphula noma iyiphi iphethini, uphawu, imfihlo yokuhweba, ilungelo lokushicilela, noma ezinye izimfanelo zomuntu.
      • Kukhuthaza imisebenzi engemthetho noma ukuziphatha okungahloniphi, okuthusayo, okungcolile, noma okudumazayo.
    • Sigcina ilungelo lokubuyekeza, ukubheka, nokukhipha noma yikuphi okuqukethwe okuphula le Migomo.
  5. Amalungelo Obuhlakani
    • Konke okuqukethwe, izici, kanye nemisebenzi ye-AiCasso kuyimpahla eyingqayizivele ye-AiCasso kanye nabavumelwano bayo.
    • Unikezwa ilayisense elilinganiselwe yokusebenzisa uhlelo ngokwakho, okungelona elokuhweba.
  6. Imisebenzi Evinjelwe
    • Uyavuma ukuthi ungakwenzi:
      • Shintsha, uhlele, uphule, noma wenze i-reverse engineering noma iyiphi ingxenye yehlelo.
      • Usebenzise uhlelo nganoma iyiphi indlela engemthetho noma engavunyelwe.
      • Layisha noma ukukhiqiza noma yikuphi okuqukethwe okungemthetho, noma okuphula amalungelo omunye umuntu.
      • Phula noma yiziphi izinyathelo esingasebenzisa ukuvimbela noma ukuvinjwa kokufinyelela kuhlelo.
  7. Ukupheliswa Kwe-akhawunti
    • Sigcina ilungelo lokuphelisa noma ukuvimbela ukufinyelela kwakho ku-AiCasso ngokukhetha kwethu, ngaphandle kwesaziso, ngenxa yokuziphatha esikholwa ukuthi kuphula le Migomo noma kubangela umonakalo kwabanye abasebenzisi noma ezindaweni zangaphandle.
    • Uma i-akhawunti yakho ipheliswa ngenxa yokuphula le Migomo, ungabuyela emuva ukuze ubhalise izinsizakalo zethu.
  8. Izaziso
    • i-AiCasso inikezwa "njengoba ikhona" ngaphandle kwezivumelwano zohlobo oluthile.
    • Asiqinisekisi ukuthi uhlelo luzoba ngaphandle kwephutha noma lungaphazamiseki.
  9. Ukuncishiswa Kwecala
    • Nakuphi lapho, i-AiCasso ayinakubhekelela noma yiziphi izingozi, izenzakalo, ezikhethekile, noma imiphumela ephuma ekusebenziseni kwakho uhlelo.
  10. Umthetho Ophakanyisiwe
    • Le Migomo Yokusebenzisa iphethwe futhi ihlaziywa ngokuhambisana nemithetho efanele endaweni yakho.
  11. Izinguquko KwiMigomo
    • Singabuyekeza le Migomo Yokusebenzisa ngezikhathi ezithile. Izinguquko zizoba namandla ngokushesha uma zishicilelwa.
    • Ukusebenzisa kwakho okuqhubekayo kwe-AiCasso ngemuva kwezinguquko kukhombisa ukuvuma kwakho kwemigomo emisha.
