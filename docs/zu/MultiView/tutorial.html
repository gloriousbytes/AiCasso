Umhlahlandlela Wokusebenzisa Isici Se-Multi View

Isici se-Multi View ku-AiCasso sikuvumela ukuthi ubheke into ezindaweni ezahlukene ngokwakha imibono eminingi yento esithombeni sakho. Lokhu kusebenza kahle kakhulu uma ufuna ukuqonda kangcono ama-shape e-3D noma ukukhombisa imikhiqizo. Lesi sici sisebenza kahle uma into ingenayo i-background, ngakho qiniseka ukususa noma yiziphi izinto eziphazamisa ukuze uthole imiphumela engcono.

Nazi ezinye zezinto ongazenza ukuze uqiniseke ukuthi isithombe sakho sitholakala kahle:

  • Into Engenayo I-Background: Isici se-Multi View sisebenza kahle kakhulu uma into esithombeni sakho ihlukaniswe ne-background. Lokhu kuvumela i-AiCasso ukuthi igxile kuphela kwento futhi ikhiqize imibono ecacile.
  • Ukukhanya Okuhle: Qiniseka ukuthi into yakho ikhanyisiwe kahle esithombeni sokufaka, njengoba imithunzi nokukhanya okungalingani kungathinta ukuthi into ibukeka kanjani ezindaweni ezahlukene.
  • Izinto Eziningi: Uma kukhona izinto ezingaphezu kweyodwa esithombeni, zama ukuhlukanisa leyo ofuna ukuyibona ezindaweni ezahlukene noma ukhethe isithombe esilula esinento eyodwa kuphela.
