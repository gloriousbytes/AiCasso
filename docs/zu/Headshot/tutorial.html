Izinketho Ezingeziwe

Ufuna ukwengeza okuthile okuthokozisayo noma ukulawula kumakhanda akho akhiqiziwe? Nansi eminye imiklamo emihle ongayishintsha:

  • Ukukhuthaza Okubi: Lapha ungatshela i-AiCasso lokho ungafuni ukukubona esithombeni. Isibonelo, uma ungafuni noma yiziphi izinto ezisemva, ungakukhuluma lapha. Uma uyishiya ingenalutho, i-AiCasso izogxila kulokho okufunayo.
  • Inani Lezinyathelo Zokuhlanza: Lokhu kulawula ukuthi ubuso bubukeka kanjani buhlanzekile futhi buhle. Ungakhetha phakathi kwezinyathelo eziyi-21, 31, noma 41, kanti u-41 ungumkhiqizo ojwayelekile. Izinyathelo eziningi ngokuvamile zisho umphumela ohlanzekile, opholile.
  • Isikali Sokuhola: Lokhu kufana nokunikeza i-AiCasso ukugcizelela okuncane ukuze ihlale iqinile kulokho okuchazayo. Isikali sishintsha ukusuka ku-1 kuya ku-20, kanti u-10 ungumkhiqizo ojwayelekile. Izibalo eziphezulu zisho ukuthi i-AiCasso izohlala ikhuluma kakhulu kulokho okuchazayo.
  • S Scale: Lokhu kulungisa isakhiwo sobuso. Kukhona okuthile kwezobuchwepheshe, kodwa ngokuyinhloko kulungisa ukuthi izici zobuso ziqinile noma zibe mncane. Umkhiqizo ojwayelekile ngu-1.0, futhi ungaya noma kuphi ukusuka ku-0.0 kuya ku-2.0 kuye ngombono ofuna ukuwufinyelela.

Khumbula, lezi zinketho ziwukhetho ngokuphelele. I-AiCasso iyaxolela, ngakho awudingi ukuba nekhono eliphezulu noma ubhale kahle. Chaza nje lokho okufunayo ngamagama akho, futhi i-AiCasso izokwenza um magic. Jabulela ukudala ama-headshots akho!
