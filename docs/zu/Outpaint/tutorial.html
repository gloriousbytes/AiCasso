Amathiphu Wokusebenzisa Isici se-Outpaint

Isici se-Outpaint ku-AiCasso sikuvumela ukuthi wandise isithombe ngokwengeza izinto ezintsha ezizungezile noma ukwandisa okukhona. Chaza nje lokho okufunayo, futhi i-AiCasso izobhekana nezinye izinto.

Ukusebenza Kwayo:

Isithombe Sokuqala:
Chaza Ushintsho Lwakho:

Thayipha nje lokho okufunayo ukwengeza noma ukwandisa, futhi i-AiCasso izosebenzisa lezi zinguquko. Ungachaza iziqondiso ezifana nesobunxele, kwesokudla, phezulu, noma phansi, futhi ngisho nemininingwane efana nokukhulisa noma ukuhamba.

Izibonelo Zokuhlela:

Isibonelo 1: "Engeza i-iceberg kwesokudla usebenzisa ifektha yokuhumusha engu-0.4."

Umphumela: I-AiCasso iyandisa isithombe kwesokudla futhi ifaka i-iceberg, igcina ukuhambisana nezimo ezikhona.

Isibonelo 2: "Khulisa, gcina ukuhambisana kolwandle olumnyama ebusuku."

Umphumela: Isithombe sikhulisa, sikhombisa okwengeziwe kolwandle olumnyama, ngenkathi kugcinwa umoya wesimo sobusuku.

Izinketho Ezengeziwe

Nazi ezinye izilungiselelo ongazisebenzisa ukuze uthuthukise ukuhlela kwakho:

  • Ukuphikiswa Okubi: Uma kukhona okuthile okuthile ongafuni ukusithola esithombeni, ungatshela i-AiCasso lapha. Isibonelo, ungase ufune ukugwema ukwengeza imibala ethile noma izinto.
  • Uhlobo Lokuhamba: Lokhu kunquma isiqondiso lapho isithombe sikhula khona. Ungakhetha phakathi kwesobunxele, kwesokudla, phezulu, phansi, noma emuva (okukhulisa). Isibonelo, ukuze uhambise kwesokudla, ungakhetha 'kwesokudla'.
  • Ifektha Yokuhumusha: Lokhu kulawula ukuthi isithombe sishintsha kangakanani ngesinyathelo ngasinye lapho sikhula. Inani eliphezulu lishintsha isithombe kakhulu, likhombisa okwengeziwe okuqukethwe okusha. Okungokwemvelo ku-0.05, kodwa ungakulungisa ukusuka ku-0 kuya ku-1.
  • Ububanzi/Uphakamo Lokuhumusha Ngalesi Sinyathelo: Lokhu kulawula ukuthi isithombe sishintsha kangakanani ngokuqondile (ububanzi) noma ngokuqondile (uphakamo) ngesinyathelo ngasinye. Okungokwemvelo ku-32 pixels ngesinyathelo, kodwa ungakulungisa lokhu ngokuya ngokuthi ufuna isithombe sishintshe kangakanani.
  • Inani Lezinyathelo Zokuhlola noma Zokuhlanzeka: Lokhu kunquma ukuthi zingaki izilungiselelo i-AiCasso ezenza ukuze uthole isithombe sokugcina. Izinyathelo eziningi zikhombisa umphumela opholile, okungokwemvelo ku-20 izinyathelo, kodwa ungaya ku-50.
  • Inani Lezinyathelo Zokuhlanganisa: Lokhu kulawula ukuthi ukudlula kwenzeka kanjani phakathi kwezikhombisi ezimbili ekwandiseni okukodwa. Okungokwemvelo ku-60, kodwa ungakwandisa ku-120 ukuze kube nokudlula okuphumelelayo.

Khumbula, lezi zilungiselelo ziyizinketho. I-AiCasso yakhelwe ukusebenza kahle kuphela ngencazelo yakho yemvelo. Zizwe ukhululekile ukujabulisa futhi ubone ukuthi yini esebenza kahle kakhulu esithombeni sakho!
