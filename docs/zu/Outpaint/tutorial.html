Amathiphu Wokusebenzisa Isici se-Outpaint

Isici se-Outpaint ku-AiCasso sikuvumela ukuthi wandise isithombe ngokwengeza izinto ezintsha ezikuzungezile noma ngokwandisa okukhona. Chaza nje lokho okufunayo, futhi i-AiCasso izobhekana nezinye izinto.

Ukusebenza Kwayo:

Isithombe Sokuqala:
Chaza Ushintsho Lwakho:

Thayipha nje lokho okufunayo ukukhipha noma ukwandisa, futhi i-AiCasso izosebenzisa lezi zinguquko. Ungachaza iziqondiso ezifana nesobunxele, kwesokudla, phezulu, noma phansi, futhi ngisho nemininingwane efana nokuphuma kokubuka noma ukuhamba.

Izibonelo Zokuhlela:

Isibonelo 1: "Faka i-iceberg kwesokudla usebenzisa ifektha yokuhumusha engu-0.4."

Umphumela: I-AiCasso iyandisa isithombe kwesokudla futhi ifaka i-iceberg, igcina ukuhambisana nezimo ezikhona.

Isibonelo 2: "Phuma, gcina ukuhambisana kolwandle olumnyama ebusuku."

Umphumela: Isithombe siphuma, sikhombisa olunye ulwandle olumnyama, ngenkathi kugcinwa umoya wesimo sebusuku.

Izinketho Ezingeziwe

Nazi ezinye izilungiselelo ongazisebenzisa ukuze uthuthukise ukuhlela kwakho:

  • Ukuphikiswa Okubi: Uma kukhona okuthile ongafuni ukukubona esithombeni, ungatshela i-AiCasso lapha. Isibonelo, ungase ufune ukuvikela ukufaka imibala ethile noma izinto.
  • Uhlobo Lokuhamba: Lokhu kunquma isiqondiso lapho isithombe sikhula khona. Ungakhetha kusuka kwesobunxele, kwesokudla, phezulu, phansi, noma emuva (okuphuma). Isibonelo, ukuze uhambise kwesokudla, ungakhetha 'kwesokudla'.
  • Ifektha Yokuhumusha: Lokhu kulawula ukuthi isithombe sihamba kangakanani ngesinyathelo ngasinye lapho sikhula. Inani eliphezulu lihambisa isithombe kakhulu, likhombisa okuningi okuqukethwe okusha. Okungokwemvelo ngu-0.05, kodwa ungakwenza kube phakathi kuka-0 no-1.
  • Ububanzi/Ukuphakama Kwefektha Yokuhumusha Ngosinyathelo: Lokhu kulawula ukuthi isithombe sihamba kangakanani ngokuqondile (ububanzi) noma ngokuqondile (ukuphakama) ngesinyathelo ngasinye. Okungokwemvelo ngu-32 pixels ngesinyathelo, kodwa ungakwenza kube ngokusekelwe kulokho okufunayo ukuthi isithombe sishintshe.
  • Inani Lezinyathelo Zokuhlola noma Zokunciphisa: Lokhu kunquma ukuthi zingaki izinguquko i-AiCasso ezenza ukuze ithole isithombe sokugcina. Izinyathelo eziningi zikhokelela emiphumeleni ethambile, enenani elijwayelekile le-20 izinyathelo, kodwa ungaya ku-50.
  • Inani Lezinyathelo Zokuhlanganisa: Lokhu kulawula ukuthi ukudlula kwenzeka kanjani phakathi kwezikhombisi ezimbili ekwandiseni okukodwa. Okungokwemvelo ngu-60, kodwa ungakwandisa kube ku-120 ukuze kube nokudlula okuthambile.
