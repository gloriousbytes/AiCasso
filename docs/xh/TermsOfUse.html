Imigaqo Yokusebenzisa i-AiCasso

Umhla Wokusebenza: Septemba 13, 2024

Wamkelekile kwi-AiCasso, umphuhlisi wezithombe ophuhliswe yi-AI. Ngokufikelela okanye usebenzisa usetyenziso lwethu, uyavuma ukuhambelana kunye nokubanjwa ngala Migaqo Yokusebenzisa.

  1. Ukwamkela Imigaqo
    • Ngokusebenzisa i-AiCasso, uyavuma le Migaqo Yokusebenzisa kunye neMigaqo yethu yoMgaqo. Ukuba awuvumi, nceda ungasebenzisi iinkonzo zethu.
  2. Ukusebenzisa uSetyenziso
    • Ungasebenzisa i-AiCasso ngeenjongo ezisemthethweni kwaye ngokuhambelana nale Migaqo.
    • Uyavuma ukuba ungasebenzisi usetyenziso ngendlela enokubangela umonakalo okanye ukunciphisa usetyenziso okanye ukuphazamiseka kokusebenzisa komnye umntu.
  3. Umxholo Oveliswe Ngababukeli
    • Uphendula ngokwakho kwiimifanekiso ozivelisayo kunye neziphumo zokwabelana ngazo.
    • Uveza kwaye uqinisekisa ukuba unamalungelo okanye unamalungelo afunekayo kunye nemvume yokusebenzisa kunye nokusasaza umxholo owenzayo.
    • Ugcina onke amalungelo obunikazi kwiimifanekiso ozakha usebenzisa i-AiCasso.
  4. Imigaqo yoMxholo
    • Uyavuma ukuba awuzukwenza okanye ukwabelana ngomxholo onje:
      • Okwemithetho, obangela umonakalo, ophosa umngcipheko, ohlambalaza, ohlambalazayo, otyhutyha, onobugwenxa, obonakalayo, okanye ophazamisa ubume bomnye.
      • Ophosa amalungelo, uphula umthetho, umthetho, imfihlo, umxholo, okanye amanye amalungelo obunikazi obanye.
      • Ophakamisa imisebenzi engemthethweni okanye ukuziphatha okungcolileyo, okuphosa umngcipheko, okungcolileyo, okanye okungafanelekanga.
    • Sigcina ilungelo lokujonga, ukulawula, kunye nokususa nawuphi na umxholo ophula le Migaqo.
  5. Amalungelo eMveliso Yengqondo
    • Onke umxholo, iimpawu, kunye nemisebenzi ye-AiCasso ngamalungelo akhethekileyo e-AiCasso kunye nabavuma bayo.
    • Uphunyezwa ilayisensi elinganiselweyo yokusebenzisa usetyenziso ngenjongo zomntu, ezingekho zoshishino.
  6. Imisebenzi Evinjelwe
    • Uyavuma ukuba awuzukwenza:
      • Uguqule, uthathe, ubonise, okanye uphinde uvelise nayiphi na ingxenye yesicelo.
      • Usebenzise usetyenziso ngenjongo engemthethweni okanye engavunyelwanga.
      • Uphakamise okanye uvelise nawuphi na umxholo ongenamthetho, okanye ophula amalungelo obanye.
      • Uphosa nayiphi na imilinganiselo esinokuyisebenzisa ukuvimbela okanye ukunciphisa ukufikelela kweso setyenziso.
  7. Ukwenziwa Kweakhawunti
    • Sigcina ilungelo lokuphelisa okanye ukunciphisa ukufikelela kwakho kwi-AiCasso ngokuthanda kwethu, ngaphandle kwesaziso, ngenxa yokuziphatha esikholwa ukuba kuphula le Migaqo okanye kubangela umonakalo kwabanye abasebenzisi okanye kwiimeko zexesha elithile.
    • Ukuba iakhawunti yakho iphelisiwe ngenxa yokuphula le Migaqo, awukwazi ukuphinda ubhalise kwiinkonzo zethu.
  8. Izaziso
    • I-AiCasso ibonelelwa "njengoko ikho" ngaphandle kwemigangatho yokhuseleko.
    • Asiqinisekisi ukuba usetyenziso luya kuba lungaphazamiseki okanye lungaphazamiseki.
  9. Ukhawulelwe KweMali
    • Ngaphandle kokuba i-AiCasso ibandakanye umonakalo, ayinakubangela umonakalo ophakathi, ophakathi, okhethekileyo, okanye umphumo ophuma ekusebenziseni kwakho usetyenziso.
  10. Umthetho Olawulayo
    • Le Migaqo Yokusebenzisa ilawulwa kwaye ihlaziywa ngokuhambelana nemithetho efanelekileyo kwindawo yakho.
  11. Utshintsho kwiMigaqo
    • Sinokuhlaziya le Migaqo Yokusebenzisa ngexesha. Utshintsho luya kuba namandla ngokukhawuleza emva kokuphawula.
    • Uqhubeka usebenzisa i-AiCasso emva kokutshintsha kuthetha ukuba uyavuma iMigaqo emitsha.
  12. Ulwazi Lokunxibelelana
