Uhlengahlengiso Olukhethwayo

Ukuba uziva unomdla kwaye ufuna ukwenzela umphumo omhle, nantsi eminye imisebenzi onokuyilungisa:

  • Imiyalelo Engalunganga: Ufuna ukugwema into ethile? Ungatshela i-AiCasso ukuba ungayifaki ntoni. Kodwa ungakhathazeki, ukuba uyishiya ingenanto, i-AiCasso izakugxila kuphela kulokho ofuna.
  • Umjikelo Wokukhanya: Ungachaza ukuba ukukhanya kufuneka kuvele phi—kwicala, kumacala, phezulu, phantsi, okanye kungabi khona. Ngokwemvelo, ukukhanya kuvela kwicala.
  • Isikali Sokukhuthaza: Oku kusiza i-AiCasso ukuba igcine incazelo yakho. Isikali sihamba ukusuka ku-1 ukuya ku-20, kunye no-7 njengomgangatho. Njengoko inani liphakamile, i-AiCasso igcina incazelo yakho ngakumbi.
  • Isikali Esiphezulu Sokucacisa: Ufuna umfanekiso wakho ubonakale? Oku kulawula ukucaciswa. Umgangatho ungama-1.5, kodwa ungawuseta ukusuka ku-1.0 ukuya ku-2.0 ukuze umfanekiso ubonakale ngcono.
  • Izinga Lokunciphisa Umsindo: Kukho iinqanaba ezimbini—ukucaciswa okuphantsi kunye nokucaciswa okuphezulu. Lezi zilawula ukuba umfanekiso wokugqibela ubonakale njani. Ungaseta umgangatho ophantsi wokunciphisa umsindo phakathi kwe-0.0 kunye ne-1.0 (umgangatho ngu-0.9) kunye nokucaciswa okuphezulu phakathi kwe-0.0 kunye ne-1.0 (umgangatho ngu-0.5).
  • Inani Lezinyathelo: Le yindlela i-AiCasso ethatha ngayo ukucoca umfanekiso wakho. Umgangatho ngu-50, kodwa ungahamba ukusuka kwi-20 ukuya kwi-50 ze.

Khumbula, le misebenzi yokuhlela iyakhethwa ngokupheleleyo! I-AiCasso ikhululekile ekufumaneni ukuba ufune ntoni, ngoko ke chaza umbono wakho ngamagama akho, kwaye izakwenza um magic. Akukho mfuneko yokuba ube perfect ngolwimi okanye iinkcukacha—just ujabulele kwaye ubone ukuba ungayenza ntoni!
