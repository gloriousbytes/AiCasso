Uhlengahlengiso Olukhethwayo

Ukuba uziva unomoya wokuphosa kwaye ufuna ukwenzela umphumo wakho wokuphinda ubonise, nantsi eminye imiba onokuyilungisa:

  • Umphakamo Ongalungelwanga: Ufuna ukugwema into ethile? Ungabhalela i-AiCasso ukuba ungayifaki. Kodwa ungakhathazeki, ukuba uyayishiya le ndawo ingenanto, i-AiCasso izakugxila kuphela kulokho ofuna.
  • Umgca Wokukhanya: Ungachaza ukuba ukukhanya kufuneka kuvele phi—kwicala, ngasekunene, phezulu, phantsi, okanye kungabi khona. Ngokwenene, ukukhanya kuvela kwicala.
  • Isikali Sokukhuthaza: Oku kusiza i-AiCasso ukuba igcine incazelo yakho. Isikali sihamba ukusuka ku-1 ukuya ku-20, kunye no-7 njengokwenene. Njengoko inani liphakamile, i-AiCasso igcina ngakumbi kumphakamo wakho.
  • Isikali Esiphezulu Sezithombe: Ufuna ukuba umfanekiso wakho ubonakale? Oku kulawula ukucaca. Okwenene ngu-1.5, kodwa ungayilungisa ukusuka ku-1.0 ukuya ku-2.0 ukuze ufumane umfanekiso ocacileyo.
  • Izinga Lokunciphisa Iingxaki: Kukho iizinga ezimbini—eziphantsi kunye neziphezulu. Lezi zilawula ukuba umfanekiso wokugqibela ubonakale njani. Ungayilungisa i-low-res denoise level phakathi kwe-0.0 kunye ne-1.0 (okwenene ngu-0.9) kunye ne-high-res phakathi kwe-0.0 kunye ne-1.0 (okwenene ngu-0.5).
  • Inani Lezinyathelo: Le yindlela i-AiCasso ethatha ngayo ukujolisa umfanekiso wakho. Okwenene ngu-50, kodwa ungahamba ukusuka kwi-20 ukuya kwi-50 izinyathelo.

Khumbula, lezi zihlengahlengiso zikhululekile ngokupheleleyo! I-AiCasso ikhululekile kakhulu ekuqondeni ukuba ufisa ntoni, ngoko ke chaza umbono wakho ngamagama akho, kwaye izakwenza um magic.
