Ubunzima:

Inketho yobunzima ilawula ukuba malini umfanekiso wokuqala utshintshwa sisikhokelo. Iqala kwi-0 ukuya kwi-1. Ixabiso le-0 lithetha ukuba umfanekiso wokuqala utshintshwa kancinci, ngelixa ixabiso le-1 lithetha ukuba umfanekiso wokuqala utshintshwa phantse ngokupheleleyo ziinkcukacha ezintsha ozichazayo. Cinga ngayo njengokuba 'ubunzima' botshintsho lwakho luya kuba njani.

  • Digital Art: Igxininisa kwimifanekiso ecokisekileyo, ecokisekileyo yedijithali.
  • Fantasy Art: Igxininisa kwimifanekiso eneenkcukacha, enobuchule eqhelekileyo kwi-fantasy art.
  • Isometric: Isebenzisa i-perspective ye-isometric ukudala inkangeleko enezinto ezintathu-dimensional.
  • Line Art: Igxininisa kwimigca ecocekileyo, ecacileyo ukudala imizobo eneenkcukacha.
  • Low Poly: Isebenzisa iimodeli ze-3D ezineepoligoni eziphantsi ukudala inkangeleko encinci, enejiyometri.
  • Neonpunk: Idibanisa ukukhanya kwe-neon kunye ne-aesthetics edlamkileyo ye-cyberpunk.
  • Origami: Igxininisa kuyilo olunzima, olusongiweyo oluqhelekileyo kwi-origami.
  • Photographic: Ijolise kwimifanekiso yokwenyani, efana nobomi.
  • Pixel Art: Isebenzisa uyilo lweepikseli ukudala inkangeleko enenkumbulo, ephefumlelwe ngumdlalo wevidiyo.
  • Texture: Igxininisa kwiitekisi ezityebileyo, ezineenkcukacha ukuphucula ubunzulu bokubonakalayo.
