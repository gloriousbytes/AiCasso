  • Isometric: Isebenzisa umbono we-isometric ukudala inkangeleko enezinto ezintathu.
  • Line Art: Igxininisa kwimigca ecocekileyo, ecacileyo ukudala imizobo eneenkcukacha.
  • Low Poly: Isebenzisa iimodeli ze-3D ezineepoligoni eziphantsi ukudala inkangeleko encinci, enejometri.
  • Neonpunk: Idibanisa ukukhanya kwe-neon kunye ne-aesthetics emdaka ye-cyberpunk.
  • Origami: Igxininisa kuyilo olusikiweyo, olusongiweyo oluqhelekileyo kwi-origami.
  • Photographic: Ijolise kwimifanekiso yokwenyani, efana nobomi.
  • Pixel Art: Isebenzisa uyilo lweepikseli ukudala inkangeleko ye-retro, ephefumlelwe yimidlalo yevidiyo.
  • Texture: Igxininisa kwimifanekiso eneenkcukacha, eneenkcukacha zokuphucula ubunzulu bokubonakalayo.
