Imigaqo Eyakhelwe Ngaphakathi

Ukuba ufuna ukwengeza umoya omncinci okanye ulawulo kumifanekiso yakho eyenziweyo, nantsi eminye imigaqo onokuyilungisa:

  • Umnxeba Ongalunganga: Apha ungabhalela i-AiCasso ukuba yintoni ongafuni ukuyibona kumfanekiso. Ukuba uyayishiya ingapheli, i-AiCasso iya kugxila kumnxeba olungileyo kuphela.
  • Inani LeeNtlobo Zokunciphisa: Oku kulawula ukuba umfanekiso ujongeka njani. Ungakhetha phakathi kwe-21, 31, okanye 41 yeendlela, kunye ne-41 iyimigaqo ejolise kwi-default. Iindlela ezininzi zithetha umfanekiso ococekileyo, ohlaziyiweyo.
  • Umgca Wokukhuthaza: Oku kunceda umfanekiso uhlale uqinisekile kumchazo wakho. Umgca uqhagamshelwe ukusuka ku-1 ukuya ku-20, kunye ne-10 iyimigaqo ejolise kwi-default. Iinombolo eziphezulu zithetha ukuba i-AiCasso iya kugxila kakhulu kumnxeba wakho.
  • Umgca S: Oku kukhankanya ukwakheka kobuso. Kukho ubuchwepheshe, kodwa ngokwenene kulungisa umthwalo wesakhiwo sobuso. I-default ngu-1.0, kwaye ungaya naphi na ukusuka ku-0.0 ukuya ku-2.0.
  • Isitayela: Ufuna ukunika umfanekiso wakho umoya othile? Ungakhetha isitayela esifana ne-comic, chibi, 3D, anime, okanye eliqhelekileyo. Elijolise kwi-default, kodwa uzive ukhululekile ukwenza izinto zibe mnandi!

Futhi khumbula, i-AiCasso iyavuma kakhulu. Awudingi ukuba ngumlingani wombhalo okanye ufumane yonke imininingwane ilungile. Just chaza oko ufisa ngamagama akho, kwaye i-AiCasso izakuthathela ingqalelo yonke into. Jabulela ukudala!
