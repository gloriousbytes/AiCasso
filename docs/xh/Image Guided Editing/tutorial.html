Ophumelele Kakhulu

Ungalungisa iimeko zakho zokuhlela usebenzisa ezi zicwangciso ezikhethiweyo:

  • Isikali Sokukhokelela KweMifanekiso: Oku kulawula ukuba umfanekiso omtsha uthelekiswa kangakanani kumfanekiso wokuqala. Ixabiso eliphantsi lithetha ukuba utshintsho luncinci, ngelixa ixabiso eliphezulu lithetha ukuba luthelekiswa ngakumbi. Ungayibeka phakathi kuka-1 no-8.
  • Iinyathelo: Oku kulawula ukuba zingaphi iimeko ezilungisiweyo umph工具 uzakuzenza. Iinyathelo ezininzi zithetha umfanekiso opholileyo kunye neenkcukacha ezithe vetshe. Ungakhetha phakathi kuka-1 no-50, kunye no-20 njengokungagqibekanga.
  • Isikali Sokukhokelela: Oku kuthintela ukuba i-AI ilandele njani incazelo yakho. Isikali esiphantsi sinika i-AI inkululeko yokudala, ngelixa isikali esiphezulu sithintela ukuba ilandele ngokusondeleyo oko uthethileyo. Ungalungisa oku phakathi kuka-1 no-20.
