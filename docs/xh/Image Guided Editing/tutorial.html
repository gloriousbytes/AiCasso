Ophumelele Kakhulu

Ungaphucula iinguqulelo zakho usebenzisa ezi settings ezikhethiweyo:

  • Isikali Sokukhokelela Kwemifanekiso: Oku kulawula ukuba umfanekiso omtsha uthembekile kangakanani kumfanekiso wokuqala. I-velu ephantsi ithetha ukuba utshintsho luncinci, ngelixa i-velu ephezulu ikwenza ukuba kuvele ngakumbi. Ungayibeka phakathi kwe-1 kunye ne-8.
  • Iinyathelo: Oku kulawula ukuba zingaphi iinguqulelo eziza kwenziwa yithuluzi. Iinyathelo ezininzi zithetha umfanekiso opholileyo kunye neenkcukacha ezithe vetshe. Ungakhetha phakathi kwe-1 kunye ne-50, kunye ne-20 njengokukhethwa okujolise.
  • Isikali Sokukhokelela: Oku kuthintela ukuba i-AI ilandele njani incazelo yakho. Isikali esiphantsi sinika i-AI inkululeko yokudala, ngelixa isikali esiphezulu sithintela ukuba ilandele ngokusondeleyo oko uthethile. Ungayilungisa phakathi kwe-1 kunye ne-20.
