Umgaqo-nkqubo Wobumfihlo we-AiCasso

Umhla Wokusebenza: Septemba 13, 2024

Kwa-AiCasso, ubumfihlo bakho bubaluleke kuthi. Lo mgqaliselo wobumfihlo uchaza indlela esiphatha ngayo ulwazi olunikezelwe xa usebenzisa usetyenziso lwethu.

  1. Ulwazi Esiluhlanganisayo
    • Imifanekiso Efunyenweyo: Siqokelela imifanekiso oyilayishayo kwi-AiCasso kuphela ukuze sinikeze iinkonzo zethu.
  2. Indlela Esisebenzisa Ngayo Ulwazi Lwakho
    • Ukunikezela Ngemisebenzi: Imifanekiso efunyenweyo isetyenziswa kuphela ukwenza imisebenzi enikezelwa yi-AiCasso.
  3. Ukugcina Idatha
    • Ukususa Ngokukhawuleza: Zonke imifanekiso efunyenweyo ziyasuswa ngokukhawuleza emva kokuba ziqhutywe kwaye injongo yazo igqityiwe.
  4. Ukwabelana Ngolwazi
    • Akukho Ukwabelana Nezithathu: Asabelani, asithengisi, okanye asixeleli imifanekiso yakho okanye nayiphi na idatha yomntu kwiindawo ezingaphandle.
  5. Iziqinisekiso Zokhuseleko
    • Ukhuseleko Lwedatha: Sisebenzisa iindlela zokhuseleko ezisemgangathweni ukuze sikhusele imifanekiso yakho ngexesha lokuqhutywa.
  6. Utshintsho Kulo Mgqaliselo Wobumfihlo
    • Uhlaziyo Lomgaqo: Singahlaziya lo mgqaliselo wobumfihlo ngezikhathi. Nawuphi na utshintsho luzakuboniswa ngomhla wokusebenza ophucukileyo.
  7. Qhagamshelana Nathi

    Ukuba unayo nayiphi na imibuzo okanye ukukhathazeka malunga nalo mgqaliselo wobumfihlo, nceda uqhagamshelane nathi kwi:
