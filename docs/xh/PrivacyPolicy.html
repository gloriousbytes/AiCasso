Umgaqo-nkqubo Wobumfihlo we-AiCasso

Umhla Wokusebenza: Septemba 13, 2024

Kwa-AiCasso, ubume bethu busemva kokhuseleko lwakho. Lo mgqaliselo wobumfihlo uchaza indlela esijolise ngayo kwiinkcukacha ozinikezayo xa usebenzisa usetyenziso lwethu.

  1. Inkcazelo Esiyiqokelelayo
    • Imifanekiso Eyenzelwe: Siqokelela imifanekiso oyilayishayo kwi-AiCasso kuphela ukuze sinikeze iinkonzo zethu.
    • Idatha YeMilo: Ukuba uyilayisha imifanekiso equlethe imilo, sihlela iimpawu zomlilo ukuze sivelise imifanekiso eyenziwe ngokwezifiso ngokusekelwe kwiimpawu ezikhethiweyo ngumsebenzisi. Le datha yeMilo ikhutshwa kwimifanekiso oyilayishayo kwaye isetyenziselwa kuphela injongo yokwenziwa kwemifanekiso.
  2. Indlela Esisebenzisa Ngayo Iinkcukacha Zakho
    • Ukubonelela Ngemisebenzi: Imifanekiso eyenzelwe, kubandakanywa nayiphi na idatha yeMilo, isetyenziselwa kuphela ukwenza imisebenzi enikezelwa yi-AiCasso. Oku kubandakanya ukuvelisa imifanekiso emitsha ngokusekelwe kwiimpawu ozikhethayo.
  3. Ukugcina Idatha
    • Ukususa Ngexesha Elimfutshane Imifanekiso Eyilayishwayo: Zonke imifanekiso ezilayishwayo ziyasuswa ngokukhawuleza emva kokuba zisebenze kwaye injongo yazo igqityiwe.
    • Ukugcina Ngexesha Elimfutshane Imifanekiso Ezenziweyo: Imifanekiso ezenziweyo igcinwa kwiinkonzo zethu ze-cloud kwaye iyafumaneka kumsebenzisi kwiiyure ezingama-24. Le mifanekiso iyasuswa ngokuzenzekelayo emva kwale nxalenye.
    • Ulawulo LweMifanekiso ye-Cloud: I-cloud yethu iyacocwa rhoqo kwiiyure ezingama-24 ukuqinisekisa ukuba akukho datha esele isalayo ihleli.
  4. Ukwabelana Ngolwazi
    • Akukho Ukwabelana Nezithathu: Asabelani, asithengisi, okanye sithumele imifanekiso yakho, idatha yeMilo, okanye nayiphi na idatha yomntu kumathathu. Zonke iinkcukacha zisebenza ngaphakathi kwi-AiCasso.
  5. Imilinganiselo Yokhuseleko
    • Ukhuseleko Lwedatha: Sisebenzisa imilinganiselo yokhuseleko eyamkelwe kwiindustri ukuze siqinisekise ukhuseleko lwemifanekiso yakho kunye nedatha yeMilo ngexesha lokuphumeza nasekugcineni kwiinkonzo zethu ze-cloud.
  6. Utshintsho Kule Migaqo-nkqubo Wobumfihlo
    • Uhlaziyo LweMigaqo-nkqubo: Singahlaziya le migaqo-nkqubo wobumfihlo ngexesha. Nawuphi na utshintsho luza kuboniswa ngomhla omtsha wokusebenza.
  7. Qhagamshelana Nathi
