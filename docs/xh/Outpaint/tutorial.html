Iingcebiso zokusebenzisa iMisebenzi ye-Outpaint

IMisebenzi ye-Outpaint kwi-AiCasso ikuvumela ukuba wandise umfanekiso ngokongeza izinto ezintsha ezikufutshane nawo okanye ukwandisa oko kusemfanekisweni. Just chaza into ofuna ukuyongeza, kwaye i-AiCasso izakuthatha yonke into.

Indlela Isebenza Ngayo:

Umfanekiso Oqalisayo:
Chaza Utshintsho Lwakho:

Just bhala into ofuna ukuyongeza okanye ukuyanda, kwaye i-AiCasso izakwenza la mtshintsho. Ungacacisa iindlela ezifana nokhohlo, ukuphuma, phezulu, okanye phantsi, kwaye nokuba neenkcukacha ezifana nokuphosa ngaphandle okanye ukunyakaza.

Uhlolo lweMisebenzi:

Uhlolo 1: "Yongeza i-iceberg ngasekunene usebenzisa umphumo wokuhumusha we-0.4."

Umphumo: I-AiCasso iyanda umfanekiso ngasekunene kwaye ifaka i-iceberg, igcina umfanekiso ucocekile kunye nemifanekiso esele ikhona.

Uhlolo 2: "Phosa ngaphandle, gcina ukuhambelana kolwandle olumnyama ebusuku."

Umphumo: Umfanekiso uphosa ngaphandle, uveza ngakumbi kolwandle olumnyama, ngelixa ugcina umoya wombukiso wobusuku.

Ophumelele Kakhulu

Nantsi eminye imigaqo yokukhetha onokuyisebenzisa ukuze ulungiselele iinguqulelo zakho:

  • Umphumo Ongalunganga: Ukuba kukho into ethile ongafuni ukuba ibekho kumfanekiso, ungayixelela i-AiCasso apha. Umzekelo, ungafuna ukugwema ukufaka imibala ethile okanye izinto.
  • Uhlobo Lokuhamba: Oku kukhetha indlela umfanekiso oya kuyo. Ungakhetha phakathi kokhohlo, ukuphuma, phezulu, phantsi, okanye emuva (okuphosa ngaphandle). Umzekelo, ukuze uhambise ngasekunene, ungakhetha 'ekunene'.
  • Umphumo Wokuhumusha: Oku kulawula ukuba umfanekiso uhamba kangakanani ngexesha ngalinye lokwandisa. Umphumo ophakamileyo uhambisa umfanekiso ngakumbi, uveza ngakumbi umxholo omtsha. Umphumo ophakathi ngu-0.05, kodwa ungawulungisa ukusuka ku-0 ukuya ku-1.
  • Ububanzi/Ukuphakama kokuhumusha ngexesha ngalinye: Le migaqo ilawula ukuba umfanekiso uhamba kangakanani ngokwe-horizontal (ububanzi) okanye ngokwe-vertical (ukuphakama) ngexesha ngalinye. Umphumo ophakathi ngu-32 pixels ngexesha ngalinye, kodwa ungawulungisa ngokusekelwe kumgangatho ofuna umfanekiso uhambise.
  • Inani leziNtu okanye iDenoising Steps: Oku kukhetha ukuba zingaphi iinguqulelo i-AiCasso eyenza ukuze ifumane umfanekiso wokugqibela. Iingxaki ezininzi zikhokelela kumphumo omhle, umphumo ophakathi ngu-20 steps, kodwa ungaya ukuya kwi-50.
  • Inani leziNtu zeInterpolation: Oku kulawula ukuba umphumo uhamba njani phakathi kweendlela ezimbini kwi-expansion enye. Umphumo ophakathi ngu-60, kodwa ungawuphakamisa ukuya kwi-120 ukuze ube neziqhamo ezithambileyo.

Khumbula, le migaqo ikhethwayo. I-AiCasso yenzelwe ukusebenza kakuhle kuphela ngencoko yakho yemvelo. Zive ukungena kwaye ubone ukuba yintoni efanelekileyo kumfanekiso wakho!
