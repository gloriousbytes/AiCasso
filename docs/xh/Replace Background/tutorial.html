Imigaqo Eyakhelwe Ngaphakathi

Ukuba ufuna ukwengeza umphumo wakho okanye ukulungisa iimpumelelo, nantsi eminye imigaqo onokuyijolisa:

  • Susa Kuphela: Ukuba ufuna ukususa umva kuphela kwaye ungafuni ukuwutshintsha ngento, le yindlela yakho. Kufana nokucinezela inkinobho ye-slate ehlanzekileyo.
  • Alpha Matting: Le yindlela yokuthetha "hamba neemphetho." Xa uyivula, iyanceda ukwenza iimphetho zomxholo wakho zibe mncane kwaye zingabi ne-cut-out. Ngokwenene, ivaliwe.
  • Alpha Matting Foreground Threshold: Le ikhokela ukuba zingakanani iimpawu eziphambili (njengomntu) ezihlala zibonakala xa usebenzisa i-alpha matting. I-default yi-240, kwaye ungayilungisa ukuze ube neemphetho ezithambileyo okanye ezicacileyo.
  • Alpha Matting Background Threshold: Le yenza okufanayo njengomda ophakathi kodwa kumva. I-default yi-20, kwaye ibaluleke kakhulu ukufumana umphumo ofanelekileyo nomva omtsha.
  • Alpha Matting Erode Size: Le iyanceda ukunciphisa iimphetho kancinci ukuze i-cutout ibonakale ihlanzekile. Usayizi we-default ngu-5, kodwa ungawulungisa ukuze ufumane umphumo ofanelekileyo ofuna.
