Imigaqo Eyakhelwe Ngaphakathi

Ukuba ufuna ukwengeza umphumo ophakathi okanye ukulungisa imiphumo, nantsi eminye imigaqo onokuyijolisa:

  • Susa Ngokukodwa: Ukuba ufuna nje ukususa umva kwaye ungafuni ukuwutshintsha ngento, le yindlela yakho. Kufana nokucinezela inkinobho yokuhlamba.
  • Alpha Matting: Le yindlela yokuthetha "hlanganisa imiphetho." Xa uyivula, iyanceda ukwenza imiphetho yesikhumbuzo sakho ibonakale icacile kwaye ingabi neziphumo. Ngokungagqibekanga, ivaliwe.
  • Alpha Matting Foreground Threshold: Le ikhokela ukuba zingakanani izinto eziphambili (njengomntu) ezihlala zibonakala xa usebenzisa i-alpha matting. I-default yi-240, kwaye ungayilungisa ukuze wenze imiphetho ibonakale icacile okanye icacile.
  • Alpha Matting Background Threshold: Le yenza okufanayo njengomda ophakathi kodwa kumva. I-default yi-20, kwaye ibalulekile ukufumana umphumo ofanelekileyo kunye nomva omtsha.
  • Alpha Matting Erode Size: Le iyanceda ukunciphisa imiphetho kancinci ukuze umphumo ubonakale ucocekile. Usayizi we-default ngu-5, kodwa ungawulungisa ukuze ufumane umphumo ofunekayo.
