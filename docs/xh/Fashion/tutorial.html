Ukuba ufuna ukwengeza isitayile sakho okanye uqinisekise ukuba umphumo ulungile, ungayilungisa le migaqo:

  • Umjikelo Ongalunganga: Ukuba kukho into ongafuni ukuba ibonakale emfanekisweni, khangela nje apha. Shiya ingenanto ukuba uyavuma naluphi na umphumo ovela kwi-AiCasso.
  • Uhlobo LweMpahla: Tshela i-AiCasso ukuba ugxile kwi mzimkhulu, mzimncinci, okanye iijesi. Ukuba awuqinisekanga, ibhiyozela kwi-mzimkhulu.
  • Inani LeeDenoising Steps: Oku kukhetha ukuba umfanekiso ubonakala njani. Ungakhetha phakathi kwe-21, 31, okanye 41, kunye ne-21 ibhiyozela. Iingqekembe ezininzi zihlala zifuna umfanekiso ogqwesileyo.
  • Isikali Sokukhuthaza: Oku kunceda umfanekiso uhlale uthelekisa nencazelo yakho. Ungasetha ukusuka ku-1 ukuya ku-20, kunye no-7 ebhiyozela. Iinombolo eziphezulu zithetha ukuba i-AiCasso izakulandela umjikelo wakho ngokuqinileyo.

Khumbula, le migaqo iyakhelwe ngaphakathi! I-AiCasso ikrelekrele kakhulu kwaye ingaqonda ukuba uyathetha ntoni nangona ungafumani yonke imixholo ilungile. Jonga nje ujabulele kwaye uvumele ubuchule bakho buphume!
