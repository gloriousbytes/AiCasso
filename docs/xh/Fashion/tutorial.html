Ukuba ufuna ukwengeza isitayile sakho okanye uqinisekise ukuba umphumo ulungile, ungaguqula ezi settings:

  • Umjikelo Ongalunganga: Ukuba kukho into ongafuni ukuba ibonakale emfanekisweni, khangela nje apha. Shiya ingenanto ukuba ukhululekile naluphi na u-AiCasso olukhuphisayo.
  • Uhlobo LweMpahla: Tshela i-AiCasso ukuba ugxile kwi mzimkhulu, mzimncinci, okanye iimpahla. Ukuba awuqinisekanga, ibhiyozela kumzimkhulu.
  • Inani Lezinyathelo Zokunciphisa Iingxaki: Oku kunceda ukunciphisa umfanekiso ubonakale ucocekile. Ungakhetha phakathi kwe-21, 31, okanye 41, kunye ne-21 ibhiyozela. Iinyathelo ezininzi zihlala zithetha umfanekiso ogqwesileyo.
  • Isikali Sokukhokelela: Oku kunceda umfanekiso ube sedlalweni sakho. Ungasetha ukusuka kwi-1 ukuya kwi-20, kunye ne-7 ibhiyozela. Iinombolo eziphezulu zithetha ukuba i-AiCasso iya kulandela umjikelo wakho ngokuqinileyo.

Khumbula, ezi settings zikhethwayo! I-AiCasso iyakwazi kakhulu kwaye ingaqonda ukuba uthetha ntoni nokuba awufumani yonke imixholo. Jonga nje ujabulele kwaye uvumele ubuchule bakho buphume!
