Guide to Using the Super Resolution Feature

The Super Resolution feature in AiCasso is all about making your images look even better. You just tell it how you want to enhance your image, and AiCasso will take care of the rest. Whether you're sharpening up details or giving the whole image a boost, it's super easy to use.

Optional Parameters

If you want to add a bit more control over how your image is enhanced, here are some extra options you can play with:

  • Scale: This is how much you want to enlarge your image. You can choose a scale from 1 to 4, with 4 being the default. Basically, bigger numbers mean a bigger image.
  • Upscale Model: This is like picking a filter for your image, but it's much smarter. Here are the options:
    • Big Detail Boost: Best for zooming in on tiny details.
    • Ultra Sharp: Makes the image super clear and crisp.
    • Anime Enhance: Perfect for anime-style images, making them look sharp and vibrant.
    • Double Detail: Doubles the details without going overboard.
    • General Boost: A good all-around option for most images.
  • Face Enhance: If your image has a face, this option can help make it look even better. Just turn it on if you want AiCasso to focus on enhancing facial features.

Remember, these options are totally optional! You don’t have to mess with them if you don’t want to. Just describe what you want, and AiCasso will figure out the best way to make it happen. And don’t worry about being perfect with your words—AiCasso understands you!
