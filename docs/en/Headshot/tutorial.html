Optional Parameters

Want to add a bit more flair or control to your generated headshots? Here are some cool options you can tweak:

  • Negative Prompt: This is where you can tell AiCasso what you don’t want to see in the image. For example, if you don't want any background elements, you can mention that here. If you leave this blank, AiCasso will just focus on what you do want.
  • Number of Denoising Steps: This controls how smooth and refined the face looks. You can pick between 21, 31, or 41 steps, with 41 being the default. More steps usually mean a cleaner, more polished result.
  • Guidance Scale: This is like giving AiCasso a little extra push to stay true to your description. The scale ranges from 1 to 20, with 10 as the default. Higher numbers mean AiCasso will stick closer to what you describe.
  • S Scale: This adjusts the structure of the face. It’s a bit technical, but essentially it tweaks how strong or soft the facial features are. The default is 1.0, and you can go anywhere from 0.0 to 2.0 depending on the look you're going for.

Remember, these parameters are totally optional. AiCasso is pretty forgiving, so you don’t have to be super precise or grammatically correct. Just describe what you want in your own words, and AiCasso will do the magic. Have fun creating your headshots!
