Tips for Image Guided Editing

The Image Guided Edit feature in AiCasso lets you make changes to a picture just by describing what you want. Whether you want to change the style, adjust the mood, or tweak certain parts, AiCasso will handle it for you without needing you to manually select or paint anything.

Additional Options

You can fine-tune your edits using these optional settings:

  • Image Guidance Scale: This controls how closely the new image sticks to the original. A lower value means the changes are more subtle, while a higher value makes them stand out more. You can set this between 1 and 8.
  • Steps: This sets how many refinements the tool will make. More steps mean a smoother and more detailed final image. You can choose between 1 and 50 steps, with 20 as the default.
  • Guidance Scale: This affects how closely the AI follows your description. A lower scale gives the AI more creative freedom, while a higher scale makes it stick closely to what you said. You can adjust this between 1 and 20.
