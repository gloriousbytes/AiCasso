Image Generation Tips

Creating images with AiCasso is easy and fun! You don’t need to worry about being super precise or making sure your grammar is perfect. Just describe what you have in mind, and AiCasso will do the rest. Whether you’re looking for a specific style, want a landscape, portrait, or a square image, AiCasso is smart enough to understand what you want, even if you make grammar mistakes or don’t follow the instructions exactly.

Examples:

Prompt: "Make a peaceful lake with mountains at sunset, like those fancy old paintings, square pic, no animals or boats."

Result: AiCasso will generate a beautiful mountain lake at sunset, inspired by the Baroque style, with no animals or boats, and it will be in a square format.

Prompt: "Crazy splash of bright colors, like abstract art, portrait size, no shapes or objects."

Result: The AI will create an abstract splash of vibrant colors without any recognizable shapes, following the Abstract Expressionism style, and it will be in a portrait layout.

Prompt: "Fun cartoon cat as a superhero, 3D style, landscape, make sure it’s cute, not scary."

Result: This will create an adorable 3D cartoon cat dressed as a superhero, with a playful and non-scary vibe, displayed in a landscape format.
